    • About Me

    I currently am a Ph.D student in Statistics at the University of Milano-Bicocca. Prior to joining Bicocca University, I graduated in Mathematical Engineering at Politecnico di Milano.
    My research interests lain in Bayesian methods to model complex data, in particular I focued on undirected Gaussian graphical models and Mixture Models for clustering. I like to place particular attention to the computational details of the problem as I am fon of C++ programming, expecially when coupled with an R interface. Check out the list of my R packages here.
